About | Kevin Rangel

I’m a freelance web developer, gamer, and an aspiring game developer; based in Helsinki, Finland. I’m a 90s kid and lived most of my life in Belgium. I studied electronics, and worked as a service technician, before realizing programming is too much fun. So, I have been working as a frontend web developer ever since.

I’ve developed websites and web applications for Freebility, Famoco, and Epic Games. In 2020 (amazing timing), I decided to become self-employed, and currently work as a consultant for Equinor.

Currently, I devote most of my time to web development, but in the future I want to focus on game development topics. Additionally, I wish to write blog posts using the learning notes from my second brain.
